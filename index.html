About Me

I'm currently a fourth year undergraduate student at the Department of Computer Science IIT-Madras. My research interests are mainly in Computer Graphics and Deep Learning. I've been dabbling in the field of Computer Graphics since 10th grade, primarily motivated by video games and movie VFX. I believe that the movies are soon likely to be completely rendered, which will allow for styles of cinematography that we haven't heard of yet (The Adventures of Tintin 2015 is proof of this). I also work in the field of Reinforcement Learning, where I use concepts from Deep Learning to build agents that can solve tasks where the inputs are mainly images. I've also worked with several startups in my freshman and sophomore years. I co-founded one startup, Clozerr, that would go on to become the first startup to be incubated with only undergraduate cofounders.

I love travelling (mainly backpacking, but not the serious kind where you have no money...) and photography (although not very good at it!)
